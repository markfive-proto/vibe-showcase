Tuscan Chicken Pasta

A rich, creamy one-pan dish that brings the warmth of Tuscany to your table.

35 min 4 servings Easy

Nutrition per serving

Calories520
Fat22g
Protein32g
Carbs48g
Fiber4g
Sodium680mg

Ingredients

  • 500g penne pasta
  • 4 chicken thighs, diced
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes, drained
  • 3 cups fresh spinach
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1 Cook penne according to package directions. Drain and set aside, reserving 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. 2 Season chicken with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat and cook chicken until golden, about 6-7 minutes.
  3. 3 Add minced garlic and sun-dried tomatoes. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. 4 Pour in heavy cream and stir. Let simmer for 3-4 minutes until slightly thickened.
  5. 5 Add spinach and stir until wilted. Mix in parmesan cheese and cooked pasta. Add pasta water if needed.
  6. 6 Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and extra parmesan.
